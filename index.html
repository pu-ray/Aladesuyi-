About Me

Hello!, I'm Oyindamola Nerfatiti Aladesuyi an award wining Nigerian model, actor, fashion designer, artist content creator and humanitarian.

I was born in Ekiti state Nigeria to Mr Bayo and Mrs Mercilina Aladesuyi, a graduate of English and literary studies from federal university Oye Ekiti, I worn several awards in my primary school days as the best female painter also represented my school in inter states art competitions. I started my modeling career in 2012, also contested for Nigeria Next Super Model in 2015

I won Miss Bikini Nigeria in 2016 and walked the runway for top Nigerian designers and featured in top Nigerian music videos with Nigerian top artistes. I am also part of the Art For Change Africa NGO, an organization that promotes the works of young talented less privileged teenagers and youths from the slum.
